Introduction

We are pleased to present our project, UniTapqyr AI ‚Äî an intelligent ecosystem designed to transform the university admission process in Kazakhstan through advanced predictive analytics and AI-driven personalization.The project addresses the problem of informational gaps and high levels of stress among applicants caused by complex admission regulations and uncertainty in evaluating their chances of obtaining a state-funded grant.


How did we come up with this idea?

As recent graduates ourselves, we have firsthand experience with the immense pressure of the university admission process. We understand the 'human side' of this challenge: the paralyzing stress, the anxiety of high expectations, and the uncertainty caused by complex grant regulations. Many students struggle not because of a lack of talent, but due to a lack of clear information and the inability to objectively assess their competitiveness. UniTapqyr AI was born from this personal struggle. Our mission is to eliminate this psychological strain by providing students with a data-driven 'roadmap' to their future. We are not just building an app; we are creating a support system that gives every applicant the confidence in their future they deserve.


Our Primary Objectives:

To simplify access to university admission information.

This objective focuses on structuring complex admission requirements, deadlines, and criteria into a clear and understandable format for students.

To provide personalized admission recommendations.

The system analyzes academic indicators (UNT scores, IELTS results, GPA, achievements) to suggest suitable universities based on individual competitiveness.

To reduce stress and uncertainty during the application process.

By estimating admission probability and tracking important deadlines, the platform helps students make informed and confident decisions.

Core Functions of UniTapqyr AI

Academic Profile Analysis: Comprehensive analysis of ENT, IELTS, and GPA.
Personalized University Matching: Calculate your chances of getting a grant.

Deadline Monitoring System: Never miss a university application window.
